Wedding Cake Consultations

Let's sit down together and bring your dream wedding cake to life

Your Free Consultation

We believe every wedding cake should be as unique as the couple it celebrates. Our complimentary consultation is the perfect opportunity to discuss your vision, explore design ideas, and sample our delicious flavours.

Whether you have a clear picture of your dream cake or you're looking for inspiration, Vee will guide you through every step of the process to create something truly remarkable.

1

Get in Touch

Contact us via phone, email, or WhatsApp to book your consultation. We'll find a time that works perfectly for you.

2

Design & Taste

Discuss your wedding theme, colour scheme, and preferences. Sample our range of exquisite flavours and fillings.

3

Confirm & Create

We'll finalise your design and schedule. Sit back and relax while we bring your perfect wedding cake to life.

What to Expect

During your consultation, we'll cover:

Your wedding date, venue & theme
Number of guests & servings needed
Design preferences & inspiration
Flavour selection & tasting
Dietary requirements
Pricing & booking details
