Cupcake Flavours

Each cupcake is freshly baked and topped with a generous swirl of flavoured buttercream. Available individually or in boxes of 6, 12, or 24.

Vanilla
Classic vanilla sponge with vanilla buttercream
Chocolate
Rich chocolate sponge with chocolate buttercream
Red Velvet
Red velvet sponge with cream cheese frosting
Lemon
Lemon sponge with lemon buttercream and zest
Salted Caramel
Caramel sponge with salted caramel buttercream and drizzle
Biscoff
Biscoff sponge with Biscoff buttercream and crumbled biscuit
Strawberry
Strawberry sponge with strawberry buttercream
White Chocolate
White chocolate sponge with white chocolate buttercream
Coconut
Coconut sponge topped with coconut buttercream and flakes
Oreo
Cookies & cream sponge with Oreo buttercream and crushed Oreo
