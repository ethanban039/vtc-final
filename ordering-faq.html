Ordering & FAQ

Everything you need to know about ordering your perfect cake

How to Order

Ordering your dream cake from Vee The Cake is simple. Follow these easy steps:

1

Get in Touch

Fill in our enquiry form, send us a message on WhatsApp, email us, or give us a call. Tell us about your event and what you're looking for.

2

Design Your Cake

We'll discuss your requirements, flavours, design, and any special details. For wedding cakes, we offer a free consultation with tasting.

3

Confirm & Enjoy

Once you're happy with the design, we'll confirm the order. A 50% deposit secures your booking and the balance is due before delivery.
