Delicate French macaroons in a variety of exquisite flavours and colours

French Macaroons

Our hand-piped French macaroons are light, delicate, and bursting with flavour. Made with finely ground almonds and filled with delicious ganache or buttercream, each macaroon is a little piece of elegance.

Available in a rainbow of colours and a wide range of flavours, our macaroons are perfect as wedding favours, party treats, gifts, or as part of a stunning dessert table display.
