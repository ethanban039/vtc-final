Cake Price Guide

Starting prices for our handcrafted bespoke cakes & treats

All of our cakes are made entirely to order — every price below is a starting guide only.

Your final quote depends on the size, tier count, complexity of design, flavour selection, and any custom decorations. Get in touch and we'll put together an accurate quote for your order.

All prices are inclusive of VAT.

Good to Know

Deposits & Payment

  • A 50% non-refundable, non-transferable deposit is required to secure your order and date.
  • The remaining balance is due in full before delivery or collection.

Delivery

  • Wedding cakes & dessert tables: delivery and venue setup included across Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, and Leicestershire.
  • Celebration cakes & cupcakes: collection only from Mapperley. Delivery available for 3-tier cakes and above.

Special Flavours

A small surcharge applies when selecting from our Special Flavour menu for celebration cakes and cupcakes. All flavours are included as standard for wedding cakes. View our full cake menu for details.

Dietary Requirements

Eggless, nut-free, dairy-free, gluten-free, and vegan options are available. Please let us know your requirements when enquiring and we'll advise on available flavours and any adjustments to pricing.
