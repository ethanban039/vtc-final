Our Finest Cake Studio

Based in the heart of Mapperley, Nottinghamshire, Vee The Cake has been creating breathtaking wedding and celebration cakes for over a decade.

Every cake is handcrafted with passion, premium ingredients, and an unwavering attention to detail. Whether you're planning your dream wedding or celebrating a milestone moment, we bring your vision to life with elegance and flavour.

From classic tiered wedding cakes to bespoke celebration designs, cupcakes, dessert tables, and French macaroons - every creation is made to impress.
